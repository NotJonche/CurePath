Primary Care

We’re All Smiles Over Here.

Good oral health is a big part of overall good health, and prevention is the key. Our professional staff are dedicated to providing the best care possible to our patients, providing the latest preventative and restorative dentistry so that you can smile with confidence.

We want to make your care accessible and affordable. We are in-network with most insurance plans, including Medicaid. Discounts are available to eligible patients through our sliding fee scale based on your individual needs.
